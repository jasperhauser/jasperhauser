Coverflow part 2

Sunday, May 28, 2006 21:45  
Last friday Andrew Coulter, the designer and "inventor" of Coverflow, did two great posts on the future visual presentation of Coverflow.

He created a custom disk image icon and a custom disk image background to give the application a much more proffesional look and feel. He also did a great more in depth post about the creation of the icon and the cover art he created for the icon, showing high resolution versions of his art. Andrew is clearly a gifted graphic designer.

I really hope the guys will further improve and develop their already wonderfull application.
