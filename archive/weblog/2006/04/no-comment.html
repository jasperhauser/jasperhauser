No Comment?

Monday, April 24, 2006 12:23  
I was preparing a post on how i was surprised that I had so little comments on by posts. I mean I seriously thought it was something strange. And then today I accidentally found a preference on blogger called "Comment moderating" which I turned on, as I asumed this would allow me to edit comments if needed. This caused the comments not to show up on the blog until I allowed it manually. Argh I suck. So I turned that off, and like magic I had dozens of comments I didn't even know existed. I think I'll stop asuming things about blogger now :D

So I'd like to apologize for that funkiness, I made sure they are now always shown. And I will try and post some answers to some of the comments that where made.

On another note I'd like to say that I'm preparing a whole bunch of blog posts, of which some are about the creation of icons and UI.

Stay tuned for more.
