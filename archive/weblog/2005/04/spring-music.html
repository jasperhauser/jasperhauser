Spring music

Friday, April 15, 2005 11:07  
Last Sunday I went to my father who lives in the south of France in a small town called Sete. He paid my ticket and all so I could help him record and mix the music he had written for a new documentary. He bought a nice G4 iBook and a Digidesign MBox with Protools. All in all it was super, Protools used to be very buggy but for what we did we didn't encounter any issues. We didn't have any problems except running out of cpu from all the EQ's and the dozens of tracks we had. Good fun and my father was thrilled with all the possibilities and the fact that it was such a relaxed way of working.

I came back last night after 8 hours of train and a nice 4 hour stop in Paris for a decent lunch. I came from a sunny beach town to the grey city, juck. I wish I could have stayed longer.

Next week I will be going to the MacExpo here in Amsterdam, I got free tickets :) And today I will be going to my local Apple dealer to sign up for my copy of Tiger. I can't wait to play around with it on my iBook.
