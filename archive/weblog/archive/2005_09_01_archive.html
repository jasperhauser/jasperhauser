A nano review.

Monday, September 19, 2005 23:04  

Last Thursday I finally got my iPod nano delivered, which in itself was hard enough. Why do those people always come when you aren't around. And then when I had to make an appointment they said; "We can only ensure we will come between 09:00 and 17:00". Come on that's just ridiculous.

Packaging
So anyway I received it and as usual I was impressed with the design of the packaging. They did all kinds of cool stuff with special materials most people wont even notice, and I won't bother annoying you with the details. Best thing is that the box has two lids and the nano is in fact placed in the thinnest of the two. All in the name of impact, well done guys. I love the details.

Handling
The nano is small, I mean really really small. It makes me think of a slightly large credit card. It's just amazing. The materials feel great, the metal and plastic are nice and smooth but sticky enough to not slip out of your hands. And amazingly you can hardly feel the seem between the plastic and the metal. The controls are as we know, simply good. I always thought design of the click and scroll wheel was a marvel. I'd love to know who came up with that idea instead of just buttons.

Battery
The battery of this ipod is the best I have seen so far. I charged it and have been using it on and off for 4 days now, and just this afternoon it indicated low power for the first time. Chapeau for that piece of engineering. It must be the lack of "moving" parts that ensure this great use of power.

Features
With the nano Apple for the first time introduced all the adult iPod features for the low end and even added some new ones even the expensive ipods don't have. That and the price make it a killer product.

The nano clearly is the result of a long line of iPods. There is nothing on the market that is as well crafted, detailed, feature rich and easy to use then the iPod nano. Some will want more storage capacity but for those that don't need that this is the perfect product.

Apple i news.

Wednesday, September 07, 2005 21:24  

So Apple just released yet another killer iPod product, at least that is what I think it is. I really hadn't anticipated this tiny beauty. Seeing it it's actually what I had always been hoping for. The ipod mini was small I have to admit, but this nano iPod is just incredibly small, especially considering the fact it has all the features of the "regular" iPod. At this size it's just what I had hopped the shuffle to be. Thank you apple for this amazing piece of design. I can't wait for mine to arrive;)


And then Apple also released the new version of iTunes. Now don't get me wrong, I love the new look. Much simpler, compact and much more smooth, metal is so pass√© :D But why oh why do we need yet another theme Apple? I'm all for new design really, I just think that theme consistency within a system and applications is a really good thing. Please boys and girls at the UI team, I know it's fun to design new themes and they do look good, but get a grip and bring back some of that consistency.
