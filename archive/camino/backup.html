This little application written in Applescript enables you to backup your Bookmarks, Preferences and Cookie preference files of (Chimera) Navigator.

This application was originaly made for those (Chimera) Navigator users that want to stay on the bleeding edge of development and have to backup and restore there Bookmarks, Preferences and Cookie preferences manualy every time. And I know it takes a lot of mouse clicks to do that. But ofcourse anybody with the desire to backup can use it.

This little script automates the whole backup proces for you. It will backup your files to a folder (whose name is the date on which the backup was done). And it will enable you to restore your files from this folder. How easy could it be?

Pleas give it a try, and see what you think of it. Mail me your likes and dislikes at info@jasperhauser.nl.
