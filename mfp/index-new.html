Rob Hauser has, besides directing productions, 30+ years of experience composing music for documentaries, radio & TV-shows, motion pictures, theatre shows, operas and ballets.

He was born March 13 1954 in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. At the age of 14 he became a bariton saxophone player in the local soul band Side by Side. After 6 years, in 1972, he co-founded and became a member of the music theatre group called Hauser Orkater which, during it's 10 year existance, gathered an enourmous following and sucess both internationally and in the Netherlands. In France, the production “Zie de mannen vallen” received first price for best foreign performance in 1980.

At the same time, he started studying classica musique at HKU University of the Arts Utrecht. He graduated in 1979 as composer, and in 1980 as solist (saxophone). Later, he taught for over 12 years at the same conservatory. Since 1985 he has bee a member of the Amsterdam Saxophone Quartet playing worldwide, giving master classes, and making many albums.
